User guide
Setup steps:
    1 Download the folder with files to your computer.
    2 Open the index.html file in a browser (of your choice).
Registration form:
    3 Fill in the name field.
    4 Fill in the email field.
    5 Click on the create user button.
Game Steps:
    6 Each level contains 5 clicks.
    7 Click on the enemy 5 times.
    8 After the level is completed, a window appears with information about the completion of the level.
    9 To continue the game, you need to click on the information window.
    10 At the end of the game, a window appears with information about the end of the game and the total number of clicks per game.
